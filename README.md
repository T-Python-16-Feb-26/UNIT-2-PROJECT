# Project: Saudi Tourism Website

##  Overview:
The **Saudi Tourism Website** is a web application designed to showcase the beauty and diversity of tourism in Saudi Arabia.  
It highlights major cities and provides detailed information about their **history, landmarks, and events**.

The goal of this project is to offer users an engaging and informative experience while exploring different tourist destinations in the Kingdom.

---

## Project Goals:
- Introduce major tourist cities in Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦  
- Highlight historical and cultural landmarks  
- Showcase important events and festivals  
- Provide a modern and user-friendly web experience  

---

## Cities Included:
- Riyadh  
- Makkah  
- Madinah  
- Jeddah  
- Qassim  
- AlUla  

---

##  Features:
Each city page includes:

- **History Section**  
  A brief overview of the city's historical background  

- **Famous Landmarks**  
  Key tourist attractions and places to visit  

- **Events & Activities**  
  Major festivals and activities in the city  

- **Images**  
  Visual representation of locations
