Signature Dishes

The Flavors That Define a Nation

← Saudi Cuisine

Saudi main dishes are built for sharing. Kabsa — the undisputed national dish — layers basmati rice with slow-braised meat, seasoned with Baharat (cardamom, cinnamon, black lime, cloves). Mandi involves smoking meat in a tandoor-like pit. Each region adds its own twist: the south favors spicier preparations, while the central plateau keeps flavors earthy and warm. These dishes are not just food — they are expressions of generosity, identity, and community.
