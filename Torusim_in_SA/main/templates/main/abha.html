Before You Go

Useful Tips for First-Time Visitors

Abha is easiest to enjoy when you plan around the weather, choose the right transport, and balance heritage visits with mountain relaxation.

Best Time to Visit

The most comfortable months for exploring Abha are usually from November to March, when the weather is cooler and better for walking outdoors.

directions_car

Getting Around

Taxis and ride-hailing apps are practical for city travel, especially when moving between the Green Mountain, Rijal Alma, and malls.

schedule

Plan Your Day

Heritage visits are great in the morning, while the Green Mountain and mountain ridge areas are ideal later in the day and at sunset.

travel

Travel Comfort

Wear comfortable shoes, carry water, and plan some indoor breaks for midday, especially in warmer months.

Quick visitor tips

✓

Choose cooler months for walking around Rijal Alma and the Green Mountain.

✓

Use ride-hailing or taxis to move easily between major attractions.

✓

Plan heritage spots in the morning and mountain spots around sunset.

✓

Keep indoor attractions in mind for hot midday hours.

Ideal Abha rhythm

A balanced Abha day often starts with heritage in Rijal Alma, continues with lunch or a museum stop, and ends with a sunset walk on the Green Mountain or a relaxed evening by the sea.
