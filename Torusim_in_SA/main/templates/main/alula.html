Before You Go

Essential Tips for Desert Explorers

AlUla shines in cooler months; book tours in advance, prepare for remote sites, and blend heritage visits with outdoor adventures.

Cooler season Guided transport Site hopping Desert prep
wb_sunny

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers mild 20-25°C days, perfect for outdoor sites; avoid summer heat above 40°C.

directions_car

Getting Around

Use official shuttles, taxis, or guided tours as sites are spread out; rent 4x4 for flexibility; no public metro.

schedule

Plan Your Day

Mornings for Hegra/Old Town, afternoons for shaded sites like Maraya, evenings for Elephant Rock sunsets.

eco

Desert Comfort

Wear layers, sunscreen, hats; carry water; book permits/tickets online; respect no-drone zones.

Quick visitor tips

✓

Secure e-visa and site permits in advance via Experience AlUla app.

✓

Opt for guided tours at Hegra and Dadan for deeper insights.

✓

Combine multiple sites daily with official transport passes.

✓

Stay hydrated; visit during Winter at Tantora season for events.
