CORE CONCEPTS

What Actually Matters

Three metrics that separate casual players from champions. Understanding them is the first step to optimizing your setup.

Ping

ROUND-TRIP TIME

Ping measures the round-trip time...

Pro Level < 20ms
Playable 20–60ms
Unplayable > 60ms
Gaming Impact: High ping causes delayed reactions — your character moves after you press the button. In a 1v1 duel, even 50ms can mean the difference between a kill and a death.

Latency

INPUT-TO-ACTION DELAY

Latency is the total delay...

Pro Level < 30ms
Playable 30–80ms
Unplayable > 80ms
Gaming Impact: High latency makes the game feel 'sluggish'. Aiming, building, and reacting all suffer. Pro players demand sub-30ms latency to perform at peak level.

Stability

PACKET LOSS & JITTER

Stability refers to connection consistency...

Pro Level < 20ms
Playable 80–95%
Unplayable > 80%
Gaming Impact: An unstable connection causes rubber-banding, teleporting enemies, and missed shots. A 5% packet loss rate can make a 20ms ping feel like 200ms.
