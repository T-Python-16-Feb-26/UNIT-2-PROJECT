Saudi Arabia is rapidly becoming a global hub for esports, investing in infrastructure, talent, and innovation.

Under the visionary leadership of Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, Saudi Arabia has embarked on an ambitious journey to become a global hub for esports and gaming. This isn't just about entertainment—it's about economic diversification, youth empowerment, and positioning the Kingdom at the forefront of the digital economy.

The Saudi Esports Federation, established in 2017, serves as the driving force behind this transformation, creating opportunities for millions of young Saudis to turn their passion for gaming into professional careers.
