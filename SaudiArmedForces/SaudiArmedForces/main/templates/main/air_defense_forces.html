Overview

Royal Saudi Air Defense Forces

The Royal Saudi Air Defense Forces are one of the main branches of the Saudi Armed Forces, responsible for defending vital areas, sensitive points, and military formations against any aerial or missile threat, while maintaining the safety and security of the Kingdom’s airspace through integrated defense systems.

This force relies on advanced missile platforms, radar networks, and command systems to detect, track, and intercept hostile targets with speed and precision. It works in close coordination with the Royal Saudi Air Force and in cooperation with the other branches of the armed forces to protect the stability, security, and resources of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Through modernization, continuous training, and operational development, the Air Defense Forces seek to build and strengthen a modern and effective air defense force capable of rapid response and immediate reaction, protecting the nation, its interests, and its strategic assets from external threats.
