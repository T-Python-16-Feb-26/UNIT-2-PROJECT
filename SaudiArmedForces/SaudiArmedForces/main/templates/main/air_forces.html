Overview

Royal Saudi Air Force

The Royal Saudi Air Force (RSAF) is one of the main branches of the Saudi Armed Forces, responsible for protecting the Kingdom’s airspace and ensuring national security. Since its establishment, the Air Force has evolved into a highly advanced and capable military organization equipped with modern aircraft, sophisticated defense systems, and highly trained personnel.

The RSAF plays a critical role in maintaining air superiority, conducting surveillance and reconnaissance missions, and supporting joint military operations across all branches of the armed forces. It operates a wide range of advanced fighter jets, support aircraft, and radar systems to ensure effective control of the Kingdom’s airspace.

In addition to its defensive role, the Air Force contributes to regional stability and participates in strategic operations when required. Continuous development, training, and modernization efforts ensure that the RSAF remains prepared to face current and future challenges.

Leadership

Military Leadership

  • Lieutenant General Turki bin Bandar bin Abdulaziz currently serves as the Commander of the Royal Saudi Air Force. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Aeronautical Sciences from King Faisal Air Academy in Riyadh, and a Master's degree in Command and Staff with honors from the United States Marine Corps Command and Staff College in Quantico.
