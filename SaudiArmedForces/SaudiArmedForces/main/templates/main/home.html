Ministry of Defense

Saudi Ministry of Defense

The Ministry of Defense is one of the main sovereign institutions in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It is responsible for protecting the nation, preserving its security, and strengthening its defense readiness through advanced military systems and specialized armed branches.

The Ministry consists of several major branches that work together in an integrated structure, including the Land Forces, Air Force, Naval Forces, Air Defense Forces, and Strategic Missile Force.
