Overview

Strategic Missile Force

Known as the “Saqr Project”, named in honor of the founding King Abdulaziz – may he rest in peace – the force marked a major turning point in the Kingdom’s defense strategy after its completion.

The force operates advanced missile systems and launch platforms to ensure rapid response, high readiness, and effective strategic deterrence. It works alongside other branches of the Armed Forces to strengthen peace, prevent aggression, and protect the nation from potential threats.

Through continuous modernization, training, and development, the Strategic Missile Force continues to enhance its capabilities in achieving national and regional security and stability.

Leadership

Strategic Leadership

  • Lieutenant General Jarallah bin Mohammed Al-Aluwait Lieutenant General Jarallah bin Mohammed Al-Aluwait currently serves as the Commander of the Royal Saudi Strategic Missile Force. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Military Sciences from King Abdulaziz Military College in Riyadh, a Bachelor's degree in Public Administration from King Abdulaziz University in Jeddah, and a Master's degree in Military Sciences from the Command and Staff College of the Armed Forces in Riyadh.

History

History of Strategic Missile Force

Foundation

Establishment

The Strategic Missile Force was established as an essential part of the Kingdom’s defense system, with the purpose of strengthening deterrence capabilities and supporting national security. Its foundation marked the beginning of a specialized force focused on protecting the country’s strategic interests through advanced missile operations and highly organized military planning.

Development

Expansion

Over the years, the force witnessed major development in its systems, infrastructure, and operational readiness. This stage included the modernization of missile capabilities, the improvement of command and control systems, and the continuous training of personnel to ensure a high level of efficiency, discipline, and preparedness in strategic operations.

Today

Modern Force

Today, the Strategic Missile Force represents a modern and highly prepared branch of the Saudi Armed Forces. It plays a vital role in maintaining strategic deterrence, supporting defense stability, and contributing to the Kingdom’s overall military strength through advanced technologies, professional expertise, and constant operational readiness.

Vision 2030

Future Development

Technology

Saudi Vision 2030 supports the adoption of advanced technologies in defense fields, including the modernization of missile systems, precision capabilities, and strategic command technologies to enhance effectiveness and long-term readiness.

Training

Continuous training remains a major priority, with a focus on building highly qualified personnel who can manage complex systems, respond efficiently to strategic demands, and operate with confidence in different defense scenarios.

Growth

Future growth includes strengthening operational capabilities, supporting military development programs, and aligning strategic defense progress with the Kingdom’s broader goals for security, modernization, and national resilience.

Achievements

Key Achievements

Defense Strength

The force has contributed significantly to strengthening the Kingdom’s defense posture by supporting strategic deterrence and reinforcing national security against major threats.

Readiness

Maintaining a high state of operational readiness has been one of its most important achievements, ensuring the ability to respond effectively whenever national defense requires it.

Modern Systems

The adoption of advanced systems and continuous modernization efforts have improved performance, precision, and the overall reliability of strategic defense operations.

Strategic Role

Through its strategic role, the force supports stability, strengthens deterrence, and enhances the Kingdom’s position as a nation committed to strong and secure defense capabilities.
