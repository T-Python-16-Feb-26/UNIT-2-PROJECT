In Memory

Martyrs of Honor

We honor the martyrs who gave their lives in defense of the Kingdom.

Their sacrifice will forever remain a light of honor, loyalty, and courage in the memory of the nation.

Support

Support for Families

The Kingdom places great importance on supporting the families of martyrs, recognizing their sacrifice as a shared national responsibility.

  • Providing continuous social and emotional support for martyrs’ families
  • Offering financial assistance to ensure long-term stability
  • Providing educational opportunities for their children
  • Ensuring access to healthcare and essential services
  • Honoring martyrs through official ceremonies and national recognition
Recognition

National Recognition

Martyrs are honored through official recognition, national ceremonies, and remembrance initiatives that preserve their legacy. Their sacrifice remains a symbol of pride, dignity, and service to the Kingdom.
