Abha: Nature and Mist

Abha is the leading mountain tourism destination in the Kingdom, where mist meets green mountain peaks. The region is known for its cool breeze, hanging heritage villages, and the generous hospitality of its people, making it a charming haven for those seeking peace and natural beauty.
