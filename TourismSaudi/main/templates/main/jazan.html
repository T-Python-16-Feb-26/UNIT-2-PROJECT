جازان: لؤلؤة الجنوب

Jazan is the Kingdom's maritime gateway on the Red Sea, a land of stunning diversity where turquoise beaches and dreamy islands meet green mountain highlands. Famous for its premium Khawlani coffee and fragrant jasmine, Jazan is an ideal destination for nature lovers and explorers.

جازان في رؤية 2030

Vision 2030 focuses on transforming Jazan into a global logistics and industrial hub, in addition to developing the Farasan Islands into a world-class ecotourism destination.
