Saudi Agricultural Festivals

Celebrating seasonal harvests, local produce, and rural tourism across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Saudi Arabia hosts a wide range of seasonal agricultural festivals that highlight its rich agricultural diversity. These events promote local products, support farmers, and strengthen agricultural tourism in line with Vision 2030.

Dates

Date Festivals

Held in Al-Ahsa, Al-Qassim, and Madinah, these festivals celebrate one of Saudi Arabia’s most important crops. They include exhibitions, auctions, and cultural events.

Honey

Honey Festivals

Famous in Al-Baha and Harimla, showcasing premium Sidr honey and other natural honey varieties produced by local beekeepers.

Flowers

Taif Rose Festival

An annual festival in Taif celebrating the world-famous Taif rose, used in perfumes and rose water production.

Olives

Al-Jouf Olive Festival

One of the largest olive festivals in the region, highlighting Saudi Arabia’s leading olive production industry.

Fruits

Al-Baha Pomegranate Festival

Celebrates the high-quality pomegranates grown in the mountain regions of Al-Baha.

Tropical

Jazan Coffee & Mango Festival

Showcases Saudi Arabia’s tropical agriculture including coffee, mangoes, bananas, and papayas.
