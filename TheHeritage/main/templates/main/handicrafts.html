Saudi traditional food reflects the richness of the Kingdom's heritage and culture, featuring distinctive flavors and simple ingredients that have been passed down through generations.

Al-Sadu (Weaving)

Al-Sadu is a traditional Bedouin weaving craft known for its geometric patterns and vibrant colors.

Palm Handicrafts

Palm handicrafts are made from palm fronds and are widely used in Saudi heritage to create baskets, mats, and other useful items.

Pottery

Pottery is one of the oldest traditional crafts, where clay is shaped and fired to create pots, jars, and decorative items used in daily life.

Embroidery

Embroidery is a traditional craft that involves decorating fabric using colorful threads. It reflects cultural identity and is often seen in traditional clothing.

Jewelry

Traditional jewelry reflects cultural identity and is often made from gold or silver, featuring unique designs inspired by heritage.

Wood Crafts

Wood crafts involve carving and shaping wood into decorative and functional items, reflecting traditional artistic skills in Saudi heritage.
