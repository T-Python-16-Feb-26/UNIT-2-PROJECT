{% trans "A traditional Saudi performance known as Najdi Ardah, performed during national events and celebrations. It symbolizes strength and unity and is recognized by UNESCO as intangible cultural heritage." %}

{% trans "Al-Samri" %}

{% trans "A popular folk music performance that originated in Al-Qassim and spread to different regions. It combines singing, poetry, and rhythmic movement." %}

{% trans "A coastal musical art from Yanbu, characterized by fast rhythms. It is part of traditional sea songs performed by sailors." %}

{% trans "A traditional dance from southern Saudi Arabia, performed during celebrations and weddings, expressing bravery and pride." %}

{% trans "A folkloric dance popular in western Saudi Arabia, especially in Taif, known for its rhythmic drumming and group performance." %}

{% trans "A group performance from Asir and Najran where participants stand in rows and chant poetry together during special occasions." %}

{% trans "Al-Dahha" %}

{% trans "A northern folk dance with a strong, energetic character, performed by men in groups with chanting and rhythmic movements. It expresses courage, unity, and is commonly performed during celebrations and social occasions." %}
