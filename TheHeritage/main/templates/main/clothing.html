Southern Region (Women)

Women's traditional clothing in the southern region is characterized by bold colors and rich embroidery. Black garments are often decorated with colorful geometric patterns, especially on the chest and sleeves, along with traditional silver belts and accessories that enhance the cultural look. Some outfits also feature wide sleeves and flowing designs, combined with head coverings and traditional hats, reflecting the rich cultural identity and diversity of the southern region
