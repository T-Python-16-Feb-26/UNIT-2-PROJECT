Emissions Reduction Progress

Saudi Arabia is scaling renewable energy and carbon reduction systems, including solar, wind, hydrogen, and carbon capture projects.

  • Large renewable energy pipeline under development at tens-of-gigawatts scale.
  • Carbon capture and storage hubs planned to capture up to 44 MtCO2 annually by 2035.
  • These developments directly support the 278 MtCO2e reduction target by 2030.

Renewable Energy Transformation

The Kingdom is targeting 50% of electricity generation from renewables by 2030, supported by massive solar and wind expansion programs.

This shift reduces dependence on fossil fuels in domestic power generation and strengthens clean energy exports.

Land Restoration and Reforestation

SGI supports large-scale ecosystem rehabilitation across the Kingdom.

  • Tens of millions of trees already planted since launch.
  • Reforestation programs targeting 600+ million trees by 2030.
  • Land restoration projects across deserts, wetlands, and coastal ecosystems.

These efforts help combat desertification and improve biodiversity.

Protected Areas Expansion

Current protected land and marine areas stand at:

  • Around 18% of land protected.
  • Around 6% of marine areas protected.
  • The national goal is to increase this to 30% by 2030.
